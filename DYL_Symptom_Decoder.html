Your Terrain Check
Here is what your body is showing.

Your liver is working overtime to convert protein waste (ammonia) into urea, neutralize acids, and process fats. When this chemical factory gets backed up, it cannot do its other jobs — including hormone regulation and T4 to T3 conversion.

When the lymphatic system is stagnant, waste has nowhere to go. The body reroutes it outward through the skin, or inward as emotional heat. Skin symptoms and disproportionate emotional responses are often the same message in different languages.

Clear urine with no sediment often means the kidneys are not actively filtering lymphatic waste. The liver processes toxins, but it depends on the kidneys to carry them out. If the kidneys are not filtering, the liver has nowhere to send what it processes.
