The Shift You Are Making

Why Labs Miss It
Standard markers — creatinine, eGFR, TSH, hormone panels — measure late-stage function. The terrain has been stressed for years before those numbers move. Normal labs do not mean a clear terrain.
🌿
The Two Questions That Replace Everything
1. What is the body trying to eliminate? 2. What is blocking the exit? Answer those two questions and the correction becomes obvious.
Carry This Into Every Appointment
The next time a provider gives you a diagnosis, ask yourself: Is this a name for a condition — or a name for what the body is doing? One answer leads to suppression. The other leads to correction.
